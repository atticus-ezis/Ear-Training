Why do it?

Through regular ear training exercises, musicians develop a heightened sensitivity to pitch, rhythm, and harmony, enabling them to accurately identify and reproduce musical elements by ear. This proficiency not only enhances their ability to learn and play music by ear but also improves their overall musicianship, as they gain a deeper understanding of musical structures and relationships. Additionally, mastering interval recognition empowers musicians to improvise, compose, and arrange music more creatively and confidently.
